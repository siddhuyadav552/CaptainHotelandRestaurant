Welcome To Restaurant

At Captain Hotel & Restaurant, we serve authentic North Indian flavors with a modern twist. From fluffy chole bhature to royal biryanis and wood-fired pizzas, every dish is crafted with fresh ingredients and traditional recipes passed down for generations. Dine in luxury or order online — we promise a meal you’ll remember.
